dbahnan

Hi! I’m Dylan Bahnan, a multi-discplinary product designer based in London. I recently graduated from Brunel Industrial Design where I developed a comprehensive set of skills both in practical design and technological design. I am looking for work within a creative environment with a diverse team who enjoy working towards a shared goal. If that sounds like what you’re looking for, continue reading to find out more…

Since I can remember I have always possessed a passion for construction. What started out as Duplo blocks being stacked in a tower developed into an obsession with LEGO, that I still possess to this day, and more recently throughout school and University, an enthusiasm for designing and building furniture. Building, for me, enables me to be a part of the whole process, from ideas and drawings to the practical manufacture of the end product. The challenges that arise along the way from taking an idea from a vague thought into a 2D concept all the way into a 3D reality is what excites me about being a designer.

Nevertheless, this enthusiasm for partaking in the whole process doesn’t stop with building; I have always been an avid computer user, teaching myself from a young age to navigate through programmes such as Adobe Suite to learning coding. I don’t take myself too seriously, but I do take my work seriously. Design, for me, isn’t rocket science; good design is about listening to what people are saying, and often more importantly to what they’re not saying. The enjoyment that I take from designing products means that I’m willing to work longer and harder in order to end up with a refined result.
